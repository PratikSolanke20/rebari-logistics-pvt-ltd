About Us

Rebari Logistics Private Limited

Incorporated on 5th October 2011, Rebari Logistics Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Rebari Tempo Services) is a family-owned transportation and logistics company based in Maharashtra. Established in 1991, the company began operations in Aurangabad, Ahmednagar, and Pune, and has since grown into a trusted logistics partner, notably operating integrated Milk Run services for Bajaj Auto Ltd. across Waluj, Akurdi, Chakan, and Pune.

Our sister concern, RTS Logistic Services Pvt. Ltd., also serves Mahindra & Mahindra Group across multiple locations.

Led by Managing Director Mr. M.J. Rebari and Director Mr. F.J. Rebari, the company benefits from over two decades of expertise in road transport management. Their leadership has been instrumental in positioning Rebari Logistics as a leading name in the industry.

Objective
Our goal is to provide reliable road, rail, and air logistics services to Indian industries, with a strong focus on customer satisfaction and employee welfare. We ensure dedicated customer support via customercare@rebarilogistics.co.in.
